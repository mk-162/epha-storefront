Installation Guide for Construction Equipment
Quick Protection Application
Best Practices for Field Installation:
1. Identify High-Wear Locations
Boom pivot points where hoses flex
Frame contact areas on chassis runs
Hose crossover points in bundles
Bucket cylinder mounting brackets
2. Clean & Prepare
Wipe debris from hose surface
Inspect for existing damage
Replace compromised hoses before protecting
3. Apply Protection
Wrap protector around wear point
Secure with included cable ties through grooves
Ensure protector can flex with hose movement
Trim excess cable tie length

Photo-document protection locations
Add inspection to PM checklist
Replace protectors showing wear before hose damage occurs

 Tools required: None (cable ties included)
